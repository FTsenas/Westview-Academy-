ADMISSION PROCESS

1: What are the admission requirements?

Our school requires a completed application form, birth certificate, previous school records, proof of residents, and passport-sized photos.

2: How can I apply for my child's admission?

The school's online application platform is currently under construction leaving only one application option that is the in-person application option. You can use our physical address to visit the school's headquaters.

3: Is there an entrance exam or assessment test?

Yes and No, an entrance exam or assessment test is conducted depending with the child's previous school performance.

4: What is the age requirement for admission?

The age requirement varies by grade level For kindergarten, we accept children aged 3 to 5 while other grades require appropriate academic background.

5: Can I enroll my child in the middle of the school year?

This depends on student numbers being dealt with by the school at that current period. If the numbers comply with the school's capacity policies then the answer is Yes.

6: Can students transfer from another school?

Yes, a transfer student is accepted with necessary documents, incuding a transcript, a transfer certificate and a recommendation letter.

FEES & PAYMENT

1: What are tuition fees and other costs?

Fees vary with wether a child is a newly enrolled student or not. For new comers, the fees include tuition,uniforms,books, extracurrcular activities, meals and transport while a general student's fees does not include books and uniforms.

2: What are the accepted payments methods?

We accept direct cash, bank transfers, credit/debit cards (Swipe) and Ecocash transfers.

3:Is financial aid or a cholarship available?

Yes, we provide scholarships or financial aid based on merit , financial need or special talent e.g sports, music, art.

4: Are fees refundable if I withdraw my child?

Yes and No, if a period of 2 weeks has passed then no, there will be no refunds. Always check the admission agreement incase of an adjustments take place.
